Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions. This lasagna recipe is sure to impress your friends and family with its delicious flavors and satisfying textures.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • spices and seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • egg

Step in making Lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add chopped onions and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Simmer for about 10 minutes, then remove from heat.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.
  7. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles, then top with more meat sauce. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is melted and golden brown.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

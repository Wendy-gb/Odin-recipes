Pizza Recipe

Pizza

Pizza is a beloved dish enjoyed by people all around the world. It consists of a round, flattened base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, which may include meats, vegetables, and herbs. The combination of gooey cheese, tangy tomato sauce, and flavorful toppings makes pizza a versatile and satisfying meal option for any occasion. Whether you prefer classic flavors like pepperoni and mushrooms or more adventurous combinations, making pizza at home allows you to customize it to your liking and enjoy a delicious homemade treat.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
